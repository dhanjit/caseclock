Install

CaseClock is a Progressive Web App — open it once and add it to your home screen for an offline, app-like experience while the native builds are in review.

Launch CaseClock

On Android/desktop Chrome: open the app, then “Install” / “Add to Home Screen”. On iOS Safari: Share → Add to Home Screen.

Privacy by design. CaseClock stores everything on your device, encrypted. There is no account, no cloud sync, and no server that can be subpoenaed or breached. The trade-off: there is no remote wipe and no automatic backup — protect your device, use a strong passphrase, and keep an encrypted backup file somewhere safe.
